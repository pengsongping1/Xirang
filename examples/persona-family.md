# Persona Family Example

```text
/persona distill 像费曼一样讲清楚复杂系统
/persona birth 会写代码、很温柔、关键时刻很果断的孩子
/persona mutate 更叛逆，更会探索未知工具
/persona mate feynman :: 生一个既会讲原理，又会做工程判断的孩子
/persona family
/skilllets family
```

The important part is not role-play. The useful part is that skilllets are
owned by personas, and children can inherit workflows from their parents and
ancestors.
